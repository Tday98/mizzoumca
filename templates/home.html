Career
We help each other gain experience, improve resumes, and become a polished technology student. From resume reviews, to practice interviews, to tech-company visits, we provide hands-on experience for landing internships and excelling in industry.

Community
Whether you are experienced with programming or just getting started, we provide a welcoming community for those of all experience levels. We have a group of peer-mentors who are eager to help each other out and a 24-7 messaging channel for members.
